Enjoy heritage and culture at Sanskari nagri

Things to do in Vadodara

Kamati Baug - you can just sit in the garden and paint, read, listen to music, play badminton, frisbee, etc. whatever you like. There's also a museum, planetarium and zoo there if you're interested.

Night camping near mahi river, kotna. Fun experience. Used to have kayaking too but recently closed.

Trekking at pavagadh and around. Lots of different trails here. Even driving up at night and just chilling/laying by the edge of the road is quite fun.
